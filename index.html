About

I am a senior in Computer Engineering at Iowa State University interested in embedded systems. Outside of school, I am a Part-Time Student for the Intelligent Solutions Group at John Deere. In this role, I am working in embedded software development on Section Control. In addition, I am also the Lead Peer Mentor for the Electrical, Computer, and Software Engineers as Leaders (ECSEL) Program within the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. After graduation, I will join John Deere in its Engineering Development Program at the Intelligent Solutions Group in May 2019.

Iowa State University Lead Peer Mentor

ECSEL is an NSF-funded program devoted to increasing diversity within Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. As a peer mentor, I develop activities that build a community within the program and scholars. In addition, I collaborate with professors, researchers, and graduate assistants to ensure the project’s future success. I have been in this position since Fall 2017.

John Deere Product Engineering Intern

I interned at John Deere Dubuque Works as a Product Engineering Intern with the WorkSight Solutions Group in the Summer 2017. The WorkSight Solutions Group creates products and solutions that optimize machines, uptime, and jobsites. As an intern, I was in charge of leading the manual testing efforts of TimberNavi, a forestry GIS-system, by documenting and verifying software defects. I also improved TimberNavi test bench features to allow for more advanced automated testing and contributed to TimberNavi’s automated test script library. I was able to collaborate with a cross-functional international development team and I developed agile project-management skills by leading and contributing to development meetings.
